Our Story

From our family table to yours

Hi, I'm Kiyna Emilia — a full-time working mom with a lifelong passion for food, and sauces especially. I've spent years cooking at home, and I always wanted a honey mustard that felt like a true sauce: something you could enjoy on fries, burgers, sandwiches, salads, or baked onto chicken. So I decided to create and perfect my own.

One night, while making it for a family dinner, I thought — why not share something we love so much with others? This honey mustard is made with that same homemade care, and I'm so excited to bring a little of that flavor and joy to everyone. ❤️
